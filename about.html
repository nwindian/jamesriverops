About Us

Local roots. Practical tools. Real results.

Who I Am

I'm Joseph Cochran — a software engineer working for Silicon Valley fintech by day, and a Central Virginia resident.

I've spent my career building software at scale for the financial industry. But believe quality software should be available to everyone.

That's what James River Ops is. I'm taking what I know from fintech and putting it to work for Central VA — cutting redundancy, eliminating busywork, and helping local businesses run leaner without hiring more people or buying software they'll never fully use.

How I Work

Every engagement starts with a straightforward conversation about where your time goes and what slows you down. Then I build — usually starting small with one high-impact workflow — and expand from there once you see the results.

I don't believe in black-box solutions or vendor lock-in. You'll understand what I build, you'll own it, and you won't need a PhD to maintain it.

What We Value

Straight Talk

We explain what we're building, why it helps, and what it costs. No jargon, no runaround.

Right-Sized Solutions

We build what fits. A five-person shop doesn't need enterprise software and won't get sold it.

Local Commitment

This isn't remote consulting from a time zone away. We're here, and we're invested in seeing Central VA businesses thrive.
